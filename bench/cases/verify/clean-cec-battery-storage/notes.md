### (2) Total California Battery Storage (Including Behind-the-Meter) — MW Only, With Breakdown
- **Total statewide battery storage:** **16,942 MW** as of Nov 13, 2025.  
  Quote: "California has reached 16,942 megawatts (MW) of battery storage available" — Governor Newsom announcement. [SINGLE-SOURCE]
- Breakdown:
  - Large utility-scale projects: **13,880 MW**
  - Behind-the-meter on more than 200,000 homes: **2,213 MW**
  - Businesses, schools, local government facilities: **849 MW**
- Note: Sum = 13,880 + 2,213 + 849 = 16,942 MW. All figures are power capacity (MW), not energy (MWh).
- Stated increase: "about 1,200 MW in the past six months" and "2,100% surge in storage capacity since Gov. Newsom took office in 2019." [SINGLE-SOURCE]

### (3) CAISO All-Time Peak Demand — Not Provided
