## 1. Verbatim Numerical Statements from Document

### 2015 Total Withdrawals
- "Water use in the United States in 2015 was estimated to be about **322 billion gallons per day (Bgal/d)**" [Source]
- "Freshwater withdrawals were **281 Bgal/d**, or **87 percent** of total withdrawals" [Source]
- "Total withdrawals at the lowest level since before 1970" [Source]
- "9 percent less than in 2010" [Source]

### 2010 Total Withdrawals
- "Water use in the United States in 2010 was estimated to be about **355 billion gallons per day (Bgal/d)**" [Source]
- "13 percent less than in 2005" [Source]
- "Freshwater withdrawals were **306 Bgal/d**, or **86 percent** of total withdrawals" [Source]
- "saline-water withdrawals were **48.3 Bgal/d**, or **14 percent** of total withdrawals" [Source]
- "lowest level since before 1970" [Source]

### Trends (Qualitative/Relative)
- "Nation's water use peaked in **1980**" [Source]
- "Electricity water use increased almost **400 percent** from 1950 to 2005, but dropped about **19 percent** from 2005 to 2015" [Source]
- "Irrigation water use increased by about **29 percent** since 1950" [Source]
- "About three-fourths of the water used in America comes from surface water." [Source]
